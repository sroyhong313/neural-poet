About Neural Poet

Neural Poet is a Deep Learning Model that was created to generate poems. It was trained using poetry from poetryfoundation.

It is based on Andrej Karpathy's blog on Recurrent Neural Network.

Neural-Poet is a Recurrent Neural Network (RNN) with a LSTM architecture. It was trained for 40 epochs to produce high-quality human-level poems.

Click on "Create New Poem" to get started or browse through past poems through the search bar!
